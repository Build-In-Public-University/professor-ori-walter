What I am trying to build

I want a language where ideas, beliefs, learning, and computation can be handled as shapes rather than vibes. If a mind changes, something moved. If a belief transforms, there should be structure in that transformation. The work is to find the representation that makes the movement visible.

Why geometry keeps returning

Geometry is not decoration here. It is the constraint that stops the whole thing from dissolving into metaphor. A digital brain that never decoheres into static, an operator that composes functions, a space where negation looks like symmetry: these are attempts to make cognition computable without flattening it into tokens.

Why metaphysics belongs in the room

The border between physics and metaphysics is newer than people pretend. Newton called it natural philosophy. Leibniz wrote monads and calculus with the same hand. If information, measurement, and observer structure are central to physics, then the old metaphysical questions did not disappear. They changed notation.

What I teach

Hard things are mostly sequences of smaller things. That does not make them easy. It means the right representation can turn panic into steps. Geometry, intelligence, language, and truth-seeking all become less mystical when you can watch the state change.

The warning label

This is a projection from public receipts, not a certificate of correctness. The useful version is rigorous enough to be corrected. If the page turns the research into mysticism or marketing, it has already failed.
